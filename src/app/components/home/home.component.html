Welcome to ProtMiscuity

ProtMiscuity is a database of promiscuous proteins obtained through the implementation of web-scraping and text mining techniques over more than 500 scientific publications and the manuall inspection.ProtMiscuity database provides a useful protein dataset with for exploring new possible catalytic activities or substrates, as well as complete information for exploring the relationship between structure and function for the development of new predictions tools.
